Examples

One tool, endless possibilities. Discover all the

machine learning models you can train with Lobe.

Plant Species

Train your app to distinguish between different 
species of plants to help people identify
poisonous vegetation.

Hand Gestures

Train your app to understand the movement of
your hand to create new kinds of immersive
user experiences.

Personal Trainer

Train your app to recognize different workout
positions to create an automated personal 
trainer that counts your reps.

Emotional Reactions

Train your app to react to different expressions
allowing people to send emoji reactions using 
just their face.

Interactive Painting

Train your app to respond with sounds and
stories while kids draw to create engaging
learning experiences.

Safety Precautions

Train your app to monitor when masks are
being correctly worn to help encourage proper
safety precautions.

Whale Watching

Train your app to detect marine life in tourist
photos to help conservationists map and
protect the ocean.

Beehive Health

Train your app to monitor the entrance of hives 
to help beekeepers track and analyze the
health of their colonies.

Baby Monitor

Train your app to detect when babies are 
sleeping or awake to notify parents before they
start crying.

Smart Checkout

Train your app to recognize different types of
produce to help checkout faster without the
need to remember price look-up codes.

Fire Watch

Train your app to detect smoke and flames
using remote cameras to help spot new 
wildfires before it's too late.

Scientific Research

Train your app to detect organisms under a
microscope to help researchers analyze large
quantities of scientific samples.

Wildlife Behavior

Train your app to detect wildlife behavior with 
remote cameras to track population and
migration patterns over a large area.

Equipment Analytics

Train your app to monitor analog gauges and
equipment to provide modern digital readouts
and real time analytics.

Telescopic Imagery

Train your app to recognize different celestial
bodies to help astronomers analyze large
amounts of telescopic imagery.

Aerial Imagery

Train your app to monitor aerial imagery to gain
insights into macro level changes happening
around the globe.
